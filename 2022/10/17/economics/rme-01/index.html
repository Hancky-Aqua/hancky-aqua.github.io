  • If α→1\alpha \rightarrow 1α→1, F(K)=AKF(K) = AKF(K)=AK is an endogenous growth model, FK=AF_K=AFK​=A.

# Criticism

  • For the behaviorial equation: It=sF(Kt,L)I_t = sF(K_t,L)It​=sF(Kt​,L), researchers usually use It,Kt,andLI_t,K_t,\text{and }LIt​,Kt​,and L to estimate sss. But sss is apparently affected by policies. Thus the Solow model can not pass the Lucas cirtique.
